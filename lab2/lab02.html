What is Polo game

https://www.pologames.com/issues/pg001262.htm

Polo game: are games played on horseback between two teams of four players each who use mallets with long, flexible handles to drive a wooden ball down a grass field and between two goal posts
It is the oldest of equestrian sports.
The objective of the game is to score as many goals as possible. The players move the polo ball down-field, hitting the ball through the goal posts for a score.
